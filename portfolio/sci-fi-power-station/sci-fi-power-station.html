Sci-Fi Power Station

Project Description

I created this Sci-Fi environment while working at XYZ Creative Group as part of one of their marketing idea campaigns. The scene was originally pre-rendered using V-Ray, but I wanted to bring it into UE4 to utilize real-time rendering and features.

This is the final product after porting the project from V-Ray pre-rendering to a real-time UE4 level.
