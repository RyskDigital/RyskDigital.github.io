Skyrim Steel War Axe

Project Description

I really enjoyed Skyrim and I wanted to create a weapon piece, so I decided to make a fan-recreation of the Steel War Axe and bring it forward to today's quality.

Some artistic license was taken to get a more appealing look, but overall I stayed close to the original shape and design.
