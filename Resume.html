Bio

I'm a technical 3D artist aspiring to work in the games industry. I love the process of creation, building, and bringing concepts from ideas into reality. I'm always striving to improve my skills, learn new techniques and technologies, and to bring my skill-set forward to achieve my goals.

I enjoy working with like-minded people in collaborative environments and teams, but I'm also confident in my ability to be self-sufficient and work independently to complete tasks and objectives. In the end, I want to be an artist involved in bringing engaging and inspiring games to people with the hopes of helping create impactful and meaningful experiences.
