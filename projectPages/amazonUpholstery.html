Amazon Upholstery

Project Description

During my time as a Senior 3D Artist at Amazon my team was tasked with creating digital 3D assets of many of the products sold by and through Amazon's online store. The 3D assets created by this team are used to drive Amazon.com's "360° Viewer" and "View in Room" features.

The assets showcased on this page are some of my favorite low and medium poly upholstery models that I created while working on the team.

Software Used:

  • Maya
  • Substane Painter
  • Iray

Responsible For:

  • Modeling
  • Texturing
  • Rendering
